INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Information You Actively Provide

  • Profile Information. To use the Service, you must register. Upon registration, we will collect your mobile phone number, full name, birth date, gender, the state you’re from, and your level of education. Once registration is complete, you may edit your profile, and add a profile picture and a short bio. Some of the information you provide, such as your bio and home state, will be publicly available to other users. Please use caution when deciding which personal information you share on the Service. To learn which sections of your profile will be public, please review your profile edit page.
  • Preferences Information. When you participate in video chats with other users, we allow you to star users you enjoy talking to, and block users you wish to not talk to again. If you choose to star or block a user, we will collect this information to exercise your preferences.
  • Payment Information. Use of our Service is available for free. However, if you wish to unlock additional features of the Service, you may subscribe with a premium subscription. If you subscribe to our premium subscription, we will collect your payment details. These can be either your credit card details, your PayPal account or your Apple Pay or Google Pay account.
  • Reports. When you report other users for inappropriate behavior, we will collect your report details. We may subsequently contact you to receive further information regarding the report.
  • Video Chats Recordings. If you report another user, or another user reports you, we may record the last seconds of your video chat conversation with that user.
  • Inquiry Information. If you choose to contact us through the contact form or by email, we will collect your contact details and the contents of your inquiry. You do not have a legal obligation to provide us with your personal information. However, if you choose to not share this information with us, we may not be able to provide you with our Service or some of its features, adapt your user experience to your preferences, handle your reports, or respond to your inquiries.
